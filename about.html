Bad conscience grew a new business

After years and years of throwing glass, paper and plastic containers straight in the bin in our offices. We were both suffering from a bad conscience. We worked for small enterprises who could not afford - or were not willing to pay expensive extra fees an extra recycling bin - since businesses aren't covered by most councils recycling schemes. And so - steaming from this bad conscience - The recyclers was born.


The idea

The recyclers is a London based business who is helping London's Small and Medium business to reduce their carbon footprint by collecting your recycling by bike. Get in touch and we will get back to you with a quote.


Decrease your business carbon footprint

Recycling is one of the best ways for you to have a positive impact on the world in which we live. Recycling is important to both the natural environment and us. We must act fast as the amount of waste we create is increasing all the time. Recycling is essential to cities around the world and to the people living in them.
